No need to invest in motion sensors if your door does not close. We aim at providing the largest protection with the least effort on your part.

Security is not all about computers

Doing business today is complex and threats are everywhere. We help you figure out what risks you are exposed to, and we work together to keep your activity safe.

Penetration testing

A penetration test, also known as a pen test or pentest, is an authorized simulated cyberattack on a computer system, performed to evaluate the security of the system.

Threat assessment

A vulnerability assessment is the process of identifying, quantifying, and prioritizing (or ranking) the vulnerabilities in a system. This is not only about software, a team or a whole company can be considered as a system as well.

Web Application testing

We perform an in-depth assessment of web applications in order to discover vulnerabilities caused by programming logic mistakes, configuration leaks, or faulty assumptions about user behavior. Both manual inspection and automated scans are used to identify such vulnerabilities.

Incident response

An incident is an event that could lead to loss of, or disruption to, an organization's operations, services or functions. Incident Response includes the activities of an organization to identify, analyze, and correct hazards to prevent a future re-occurrence.
